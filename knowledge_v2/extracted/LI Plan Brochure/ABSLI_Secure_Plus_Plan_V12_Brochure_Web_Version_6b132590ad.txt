In such cases We shall send a notice, 90 days in advance, to You to repay the loan amount along with the interest. For other than in-force and fully paid up policies, on the date the outstanding policy loan balance exceeds the Surrender Value, ABSLI shall send a notice, 90 days in advance, to the Policyholder to repay the loan amount along with the interest. In case the Policyholder fails to respond to the notice, the policy will be terminated.
