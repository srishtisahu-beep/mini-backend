            Plan Options
1. Wealth Secure
 
Provides you with an opportunity to invest your premiums 
and earn market linked returns, offering whole life coverage 
and the ﬂexibility for unlimited fund switches to get the 
most out of your investment.
2. Future Secure 
 
This plan ensures that the future of your loved ones is 
protected even in your absence. In case of your demise, 
the death beneﬁt is paid to the nominee, future premiums (if 
applicable) are funded by Tata AIA Life Insurance and the 
policy beneﬁt continues as is. Upon maturity, the Total Fund 
Value accumulated over the Policy Term is paid out.
3. Goal Secure
 
This solution is designed to secure your family’s ﬁnancial 
goals. In case of your demise, the nominee gets a 
combined payout of the death beneﬁt and the total Fund 
value accrued till that point. 
4. Family Secure
 
This plan is designed to ﬁnancially secure your family in 
your absence by giving them a monthly income of 1% of 
Base sum assured. In addition to that, future premiums (if 
applicable) are funded by Tata AIA Life Insurance and the 
policy beneﬁt continues as is. Upon maturity, the Total Fund 
Value accumulated over the Policy Term is paid out.
